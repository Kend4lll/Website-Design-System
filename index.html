Text Styles

Text stlyes can help keep your website uniform. Also to assist in quick element formatting.

Heading <h1>

  • font-weight: 800;
  • font-size: 70px;
  • font-family: 'Poppins', sans-serif;

Sub-headings <h2>

  • font-weight: 600;
  • font-size: 28px;
  • font-family: 'Playfair Display', serif;

Photo/Video Title <h2-6>

  • font-weight: 400;
  • text-decoration: underline;
  • font-size: 20px;
  • font-family: 'PT Serif', serif;

  • font-weight: 400;
  • font-size: 18px;
  • font-family: 'Lato', sans-serif;
